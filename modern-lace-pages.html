Questions & Answers

What should I wear?

Garden formal. Think light suits and flowing dresses — the ceremony is on grass, so consider block heels.

Can I bring a plus one?

If your invitation includes a guest, absolutely! Otherwise, we're keeping the celebration intimate.

Is there parking at the villa?

Yes — free parking is available at the villa entrance, and a shuttle runs from the town square every 30 minutes.

Are children welcome?

We love your little ones, but this will be an adults-only evening — thank you for understanding.
