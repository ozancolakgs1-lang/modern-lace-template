Our Story

From Florence, with love

We met on a rainy afternoon in Florence, sharing an umbrella and a table at a tiny café near the Arno. Seven years, two cities, and countless plates of pasta later, Marco asked — and Elena said yes.

Now we're returning to where it all began, and we can't imagine celebrating without you.

What to Wear

Dress Code

Garden Formal

Light suits, flowing dresses, and soft colors. The ceremony is on grass — we kindly suggest block heels. Please avoid white, ivory, and cream.

Getting There

Travel & Accommodation

By Air

Fly into Florence (FLR) or Pisa (PSA). The villa is a scenic 50-minute drive from Florence airport.

Where to Stay

Hotel Toscana · Borgo San Pietro · Agriturismo La Quercia. Mention “Elena & Marco” for our group rate.

Shuttle

A complimentary shuttle runs from the town square to the villa every 30 minutes from 3:00 PM.

Questions & Answers

Can I bring a plus one?

If your invitation includes a guest, absolutely! Otherwise, we're keeping the celebration intimate.

Is there parking at the villa?

Yes — free parking at the entrance, plus the shuttle from the town square.

Are children welcome?

We love your little ones, but this will be an adults-only evening — thank you for understanding.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We're having an unplugged ceremony — our photographer will capture everything, and we'll share the gallery with you!
