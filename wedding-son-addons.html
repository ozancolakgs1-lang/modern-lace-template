Travel & Stay

Getting There

Fly into Milan Malpensa (MXP) or Bergamo (BGY).
Lake Como is a scenic one-hour drive, or take the train
to Como San Giovanni and a short taxi to the venue.

Where to Stay

Hotel Bellavista · Villa Serena · Casa del Lago
Mention “Ozan & Aysen” when booking for our group rate.

Shuttle Service

A complimentary shuttle runs between the town square
and the venue every 30 minutes from 11:30 AM.

Questions & Answers

Can I bring a plus one?

If your invitation includes a guest, absolutely! Otherwise, we're keeping the celebration intimate with our closest family and friends.

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes — free parking is available at the entrance, and the shuttle runs from the town square every 30 minutes.

Are children welcome?

We love your little ones, but this will be an adults-only celebration — thank you for understanding.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We're having an unplugged ceremony — our photographer will capture every moment, and we'll happily share the gallery with you.
